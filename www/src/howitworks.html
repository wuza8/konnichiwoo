How it works?

Gone are the days when language learners had to grapple with thousands of words out of context just to start reading and listening in their target language. With this open-source program, you no longer need to go through the tedious process of memorizing a vast vocabulary list. Our program is designed to store and present only the words that are specifically used in a particular text, music, or book, enabling you to learn the words you need to understand and engage with the content you're interested in.

At this time i'm creating a database of music and anime stuff made in Japanese. You can click any media you want to listen/watch, to learn vocabulary used in this media by playing minigame of guessing and remembering what each word means.

As you progress through the text, our program tracks your learning journey and dynamically adjusts the presented vocabulary accordingly. It prioritizes the words that appear frequently or carry significant meaning within the specific context, allowing you to focus your efforts on acquiring the most relevant vocabulary. This targeted approach ensures that you're equipped with the necessary language skills to comprehend and enjoy the content at hand.

With this program, learning a new language becomes more personalized, engaging, and efficient. You can now embark on your language learning journey with confidence, knowing that you're acquiring the words that matter and propelling yourself towards fluency in a more targeted and enjoyable way. Say goodbye to overwhelming word lists and hello to focused, context-based language learning! In the future you would be able to choose which particular anime episode you want to learn vocabulary for.
